Way-finding System in Warehouse

Map Design & Signage Design

Photo of Warehouse

Background of project

Warehouse is a Hong Kong organization serve for teens and also being a historical heritage as a same time, their mission is instigate different types of artistic, hand-made and cultural heritage conservation projects to allow young people to develop creativity.

 However, their way-finding system is confusing so we tried to design new signage and map for them.


Image Description

Map Design

The map mainly used purple as a main color to match with the theme color of Warehouse, and orange is used to be the contrast color and representing the brick’s color in Warehouse. 

Illustration style is used in the map to present the youthful and flexible feeling, the drawing way is tend to be more free-form, the illustration of signage is showing some brushstroke to represent the ‘creating’ feeling.

Map Design

Signage Design

I use a vintage color scheme on the signage, and adopt an illustrated style on both map and signage to match with the aim of warehouse which is about cultivate the creativity of teenagers.
