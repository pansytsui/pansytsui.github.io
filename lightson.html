Lights On

Lights On is an anti child abuse campaign which provide assistance to the children in need.

This logo carried a meaning about we are willing to open the door for victims if they need to seek for any help. Also we are willing to be their light to light up their future path.

"SECRET' - A children booklet

The booklet is about a girl named Lily, who was abused by her father consistently but she is not allowed to spoil it. 

The ending of the story is encouraging the victims should tell the truth to reliable people, if not the situation would not be solved.

The story book is aimed to spread in the primary school student to enhance their awareness on such issue and encouraging them to seek for help in they in need.
