MOVIE TITLE REDESIGN : "Spirted Away"

This movie title is redesigned for an Japanese animation which called "Spirited Away".

I utilized the fading effect to echo with the story plot, which is about the girl - Chihiro's (千尋) name are renamed as Chi（千）by a witch . Therefore the font are designed to represent how her name is erased in another word.
