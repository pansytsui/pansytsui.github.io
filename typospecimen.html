GARAMOND

Type Specimen Book

It introduces Garamond including its history and the range of the typeface, which demonstrating headlines and text in different sizes.

BOOK COVER DESIGN

I use the floral border to create the elegant feeling of Garamond, and I try to add a crown graphic with letterpress effect to echo with the feature of Garamond, as Garamond is a royal font designed for King and widely used in letterpress in the past.

Red Wine is used as a main color because it is an elegant color used at different formal occasion, therefore I think it suits the high-class feeling of Garamond.

PAGE LAYOUT

In the pages inside, I used the wine red to be the main color because it suits Garamond's elegant and grand feeling. Also I tried to find some vintage pictures to put inside the book to make the book carried an old feeling to people.
